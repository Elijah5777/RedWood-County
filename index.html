Law Enforcement & Crime

Redwood County Sheriff's Office (RCSO)

County patrols, tickets, and law enforcement.
RCSO officers cover large rural zones, respond to 911 calls, investigate crimes, and provide backup for city police. They use dashcams, perform searches, and handle everything from traffic stops to search warrants.

Redwood Police Department (RPD)

City policing, patrols, and arrests.
The RPD handles city-level crimes such as thefts, accidents, and disturbances. Equipped with bodycams, officers maintain order, respond to incidents, and uphold local laws with professionalism.

Redwood Bureau of Investigation

Undercover missions and in-depth investigations.
Investigators work on serious criminal investigations, such as fraud or organized crime. They gather intel, set up sting operations, and collaborate with both RPD and RCSO to solve complex cases.

Special Weapons and Tactics

High-risk situations and tactical operations.
SWAT teams are deployed during critical situations, including hostage situations, gang arrests, and major public safety threats. They use tactical gear and non-lethal options like stun grenades and tasers for safe intervention.
Fire & EMS

Redwood Fire Department (RFD)

Fire response and rescue services.
RFD responds to structure fires, car fires, and other rescue situations. Crews deploy hoses, connect to hydrants, and use ladders to extinguish flames with realistic fire physics.

Redwood EMS

Emergency medical transport and care.
EMS personnel perform first-aid, CPR, and provide medical transportation to patients in need. The team responds to accidents and assists with medical treatments and surgeries in hospitals.
Jobs & Economy

Redwood Bank and Finance

Banking, loans, and financial transactions.
Players can open accounts, manage savings, and take out loans for vehicles, homes, or businesses. ATMs and bank branches are accessible for transactions across Redwood County.

Redwood Business Bureau

Own and manage businesses.
Players can run shops, restaurants, or other businesses. They handle hiring, restocking inventory, and managing customer orders in a dynamic economic environment.

Redwood Public Transit

Operate buses, taxis, and other transit services.
Players can drive public transport routes, pick up passengers, and follow schedules. Realistic fare systems and traffic controls enhance the experience of running transit operations.
